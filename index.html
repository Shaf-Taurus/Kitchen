Featured Recipe: Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • Whole spices: 4 green cardamoms, 4 cloves, 2-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • Saffron strands soaked in 1/4 cup warm milk
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes. Boil rice until 70% cooked, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Fry onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  3. Add whole spices to remaining onions, sautÃ© for 30 seconds.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sautÃ© for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they change color.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  7. Add yogurt, half of mint, and cilantro. Cook until chicken is tender and oil separates.
  8. In a separate pot, layer half of the rice, then the chicken masala, then remaining rice.
  9. Sprinkle fried onions, remaining mint, cilantro, and saffron milk on top.
  10. Cover with tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes (dum cooking).
  11. Mix gently before serving. Enjoy with raita!

Featured Recipe: Vegetable Manchurian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups finely chopped mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrots, beans)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • For Sauce: 1 tbsp oil, 1 onion chopped, 1 capsicum diced, 2 garlic cloves minced, 1 inch ginger grated, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp chili sauce, 1 tbsp tomato ketchup, 1 tsp vinegar, 1 tsp sugar, 1 cup vegetable stock, 1 tsp cornflour mixed in 2 tbsp water
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Mix vegetables, flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, soy sauce and salt. Add little water to make a thick mixture.
  2. Shape into small balls and deep fry until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  3. For sauce: Heat oil, sautÃ© onions, capsicum, ginger and garlic for 2 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, sugar and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add vegetable stock and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add cornflour mixture and cook until sauce thickens.
  7. Add fried vegetable balls to the sauce and toss gently.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions. Serve hot with fried rice or noodles.

Featured Recipe: Mango Lassi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ripe mango pulp (fresh or canned)
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cold milk
  • 4 tbsp sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • For garnish: chopped pistachios or a mint sprig

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine mango pulp, yogurt, milk, sugar/honey, and cardamom powder.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  3. If the lassi is too thick, add a little more milk to reach desired consistency.
  4. For a chilled version, add ice cubes and blend again.
  5. Pour into glasses and garnish with chopped pistachios or a mint sprig.
  6. Serve immediately. Enjoy this refreshing summer drink!

Variations: For strawberry lassi, replace mango with 2 cups fresh strawberries. For savory lassi, omit sugar and mango, add roasted cumin powder and salt.
